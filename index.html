Short Bio

“Never settle for mediocrity” describes me in a simple phrase. I was raised in a household culture of hard work and high expectations, and it has always pushed me to reach higher and perform at a higher level. I am currently studying finance in the Marriott School of Business at Brigham Young University, after which I hope to start my career in the field of

investment banking, and later pursue an MBA. Aside from my career and educational goals, I love personal fitness, and have developed a daily habit of weight lifting and cooking healthily. I also love spending time outdoors with friends and family through sports and other activities.
I am a learner at heart, and love spending my time reading, listening to podcasts, learning about new concepts, and perfecting old and new hobbies.
