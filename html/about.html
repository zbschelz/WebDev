Family Life

I was born to two wonderful parents in Laguna Hills, California in July of 2001. I am the oldest of five children, and have lived with my family for a short time in California, Utah, Ohio. Ultimately, however, we moved to Dallas, Texas where we have spent the last fourteen years.

Hobbies

As I before mentioned, I love personal fitness. This means you'll commonly see me lifting, cooking, hiking, playing basketball, volleyball, tennis, or any other sport with friends and family. I'm also making an attempt to get back into playing guitar as well as I did nearly five years ago.

Going Foreign

My LDS mission trip gave my the opportunity to live in the northest region of Brazil for close to nine months, where I made friends and had experiences that changed me forever. It was also here that I learned Brazillian Portuguese, and developed an incredible love for the Brazillian people and their culture.
